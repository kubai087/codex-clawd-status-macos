# v0.3.2

## Fixes

- Recover a system-connected ESP32 through CoreBluetooth after macOS sleep,
  even when the peripheral is no longer advertising and a normal Bleak scan
  cannot see it.
- Pass Bleak the native recovered peripheral instead of a UUID string, avoiding
  Bleak's implicit advertising scan during reconnection.
- Preserve the last successful CoreBluetooth UUID after transient failures and
  replace it only after a newly discovered device connects successfully.
