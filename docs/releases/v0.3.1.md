# v0.3.1

## Fixes

- Expire `waiting_connection` after 10 seconds so a generic WorkBuddy or
  CodeBuddy notification cannot leave the ESP32 in a permanent beacon state.
- Force every non-manual `sleeping` state to send the explicit all-off command
  (`leds: 000`), regardless of whether custom status effects are enabled.
- Preserve the existing transition from the expired connection notice to idle,
  then to sleeping after 30 seconds.
